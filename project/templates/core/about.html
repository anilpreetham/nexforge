Who We Are

NexForge Industrial Automation Pvt. Ltd. is a leading provider of industrial automation, robotics integration, Industrial IoT, machine vision, and smart manufacturing solutions. The company helps manufacturing industries increase productivity through intelligent automation technologies.

Founded with the vision of revolutionizing manufacturing, NexForge has delivered hundreds of projects across India and the Middle East. The company partners with global technology leaders to provide world-class solutions while adhering to international standards and safety regulations.

With a multidisciplinary team of 145+ professionals, NexForge transforms traditional factories into intelligent ecosystems with real-time monitoring and autonomous decision-making.
